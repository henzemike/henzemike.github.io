What I have been up to

I recently graduated from Actualize Coding Bootcamp and am excited to start my journey into the web development industry. My career started in fitness and nutrition through competing in bodybuilding events and helping others achieve their fitness lifestyle goals. I believe this background has given me a lot to offer into a new career into the software development industry and life itself.

Growing up and finishing high school I always had a passion for using my creativity and developing things, whether it was drawing, media arts, graphic design, and of course, fitness. After high school I felt drawn to try my hand in graphic design and pursued that for about 2 years, finding it aspiring and fun but not exactly the calling or challenge I was looking for in a career choice.

I ended up switching my major to sports science and nutrition to try my hand at my other passion. During my career working in the fitness and health field I found it to be very challenging and rewarding crafting fitness and nutrition plans to each person’s needs and brainstorming new ideas to facilitate injured and handicapped clients in achieving their dreams of a fit lifestyle.

Bringing my past experiences from the fitness industry into software engineering has allowed me to stay dedicated and think outside of the box when faced with a new challenge. Programing is like working with each individual client: there are many ways to reach the end goal and the first try might not be the one that works best but through trial and error I am able to master that workout routine or programming challenge and start to alter it to a more efficient and effective approach.

New-Trition

A nutrition app that allows users to sign up snd create daily meals to track their macronutrient and micronutrient nutritional values. This app was created to help users have a clearer understanding of the importance of nutrition and what they are getting daily.

  • Capstone: Actualize
  • Program: Software Development
  • Date: July 2018 - November 2018

Portfolio

Capstone: New-Trition

Landing Page

A nutrition app that allows users to create and track the nutrients in there meals to help them reconize the importance of nutrition and what they are lacking.

Ruby on Rails

Backend logic that allows user to create a meal and checks database for ingredients and if not found a third party API call is made to fetch new ingredient.

Ruby on Rails

Backend Ingredient model method to make third party API call to get ingredients nutrient data and store in a hash to access all nutrients.

Ruby on Rails

Backend Meal model method that loops through each ingredient in the meal and adds the nutrient parameter values from the ingredients model method hash to the meals nutrient varliables and stores it as a hash.

VueJs

Frontend logic using an axios call to get data from backend then creating a method to access model methods to calculate nutrient values for displayed meals on users page.

Users Meals

Users last three meals will be displayed and the nutrient values in those meals will be calculated and added together for a total value of each nutrient.
